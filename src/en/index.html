How to play online:

Latest version of the web browser is recommended. Here's the list of the supported browser (indicated by green color).

Room Creater - Click "create room" button. Then, send the Room ID (via messenger, email or whatever) to the one ("Joiner") you want to play this game online with.

Joiner - Copy and paste the received Room ID into the text input box left of the "join room" button, and click the "join room" button. Then connection process will be started.

If the connection is established successfully, the game will be started. If the upper "2人でぴかちゅ~" is selected, the room creater will play as player 1 (on the left side) and the joiner will play as player 2 (on the right side). If the lower "2人でぴかちゅ~" is selected, the room creater will play as player 2 and the joiner will play as player 1.

Notice: The connection will be established in about 5-10 seconds after the joiner clicked the "join room" button. If the game is not started after the waiting time, it indicates that the connection failed. The connection may fail if you are under a (corporate) firewall which blocks UDP ports or if you are under an symmetric NAT or if you are under 3G, 4G or LTE cellular network, since WebRTC — a technology this online version utilize — cannot establish a peer-to-peer connection under these circumstances.
